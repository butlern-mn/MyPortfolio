UX/UI Designer

I thrive on understanding people, digging into the why, and collaboratively solutioning on problems. I enjoy designing ideas, processes, and systems that provide more efficient and enjoyable experiences for the end-user.

I come to design with a varied background spanning clinical work as an RN, PHN, and operational, compliance, and systems administration work in the healthcare and compliance realms.

I specialize in solving end-user problems and designing more logical and efficient processes and user experiences in the healthcare/clinical, compliance, and learning management systems realms.
